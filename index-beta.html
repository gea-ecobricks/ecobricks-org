A Deep Solution
Join the Regenerative Movement

Ecobricking is way for anyone anywhere to take personal responsibility for their plastic. Together we can keep it out of the biosphere and out of capital and carbon intensive industrial processing. Ecobricking follows the Earth's example towares cycling and sequestration. Based on indegenous wisdom, extensive evaluation and earthen ethics, ecobricking isn't just about packing plastic-- it's about petro-capital transition.

The Problem is the Solution.
Using Ecobricked Plastic we can Build our Greenest Visions.

Ecobricks are reusable building blocks intended for building green spaces. Use them make modules, home furniture, food-forest gardens, play parks, and more. Ecobricking applications are fundamentally circular, zero-carbon and regenerative.

Introducing AES Plastic Offsetting

We live in the plastic age-- no matter how much we minimize, plastic consumption is an inevitable part of our petro-capital age. That's where ecobricking comes in-- and offsetting! Imagine if for every 1kg of plastic consumed you could support 2kg of removed from industry and from the biosphere. There's no better way to accelerate the plastic transition we all long to see.

Wikipedia Ecobricks
Also know as eco bricks, eco-bricks, EcoBricks, ecolladrillos, and bottle bricks the Global Ecobrick Alliance and Wikipedia endorse 'ecobrick' to refer to the sequestration of plastic in a PET bottle to make a reusable building block.

Earth Enterprise
The Global Ecobrick Alliance is a for-Earth, not-for-profit, enterprise based on regenerative principles.

On principle we have no corporate, government or company sponsors and receive no grants nor donations. We generate revenue by providing deep green ecological value.

We fully disclose our receipt and usage of funds on our open books system.
Our Ecological Impact
We make sure to subtract more CO2 and plastic than we add to the biosphere, to acheive circularity in all our creations, increase biodiversity in all our spaces, put ecological contribution before profit, and to fully disclose all our ecological impacts.
